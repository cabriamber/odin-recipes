Eggnog Creme Brulee

A delicious classic holiday custard, with an original twist of bourbon, nutmeg and eggnog.

Ingredients

  • 8 large egg yokes
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup additional granulated sugar (for sprinkling)
  • 3/4 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon bourbon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees Farenheit
  2. Put 8 shallow, 4 ounce creme brulee dishes in a large roasting pan
  3. Bring a kettle of water to a boil
  4. In a large bowl, whisk together egg yolks, 1/2 cup granulated sugar, nutmeg and salt. Whisk in heavy cream, milk, and bourbon
  5. Divide mixture carefully among creme brulee dishes
  6. Transfer roasting pan to the oven
  7. Carefully pour the boiling water into the roasting pan to come halfway up the sides of the creme brulee dishes
  8. Bake until the edges of the custard are set, but jiggle in the center, 25-30 minutes, taking care to not overcook
  9. With a spatula or tongs, carefully transfer the dishes to a rack to cool completely
  11. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, up to 2 days
  12. Just before serving, remove the plastic wrap and sprinkle one of the custards evently with 2-3 teaspoons sugar
  13. Pass a kitchen torch over the surface until the sugar melts and turns a deep golden brown
  14. Repeat with the remaining custards
  15. Allow the sugar to cool for a few minutes before serving
