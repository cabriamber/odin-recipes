Chicken Drumsticks

This is a delicious, easy recipe for spicy, maple glazed chicken drumsticks. It will take just over an hour to complete.

Ingredients

  • 4 teaspoons dark brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • A heaping quarter teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • 4 chicken drumsticks, patted dry
