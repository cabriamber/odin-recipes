Chicken Drumsticks

This is a delicious, easy recipe for spicy, maple glazed chicken drumsticks. It will take just over an hour to complete.

Ingredients

  • 4 teaspoons dark brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • A heaping quarter teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • 4 chicken drumsticks, patted dry

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees Farenheit
  2. In a small bowl, stir together the brown sugar, paprika, salt, black pepper, and cayenne with a fork or your fingers
  3. Rub the spice mix all over the chicken (You can also complete this step 1-12 hours ahead. Refrigerate uncovered, then bring to room temperature before cooking.)
  4. Place the chicken on a parchment lined baking sheet
  5. Bake, without flipping, until the chicken is cooked through and the skin is crisp, 1 hour to 1 hour 15 minutes
  6. Serve chicken with pan drippings spooned over top
